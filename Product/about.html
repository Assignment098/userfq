SOS Cafe, also known as (Save Our Souls), is a coffee shop based in Qatar, Al Khor City. Launched in 2019, SOS Cafe continues to satisfy their customers with high quality products. We offer a wide variation of high quality products; including hot and cold beverages, Iced tea, Sweets, Milkshakes, Sandwiches, and many more to make sure you have the best coffee shop experience! Whether it’s for one, two, or twenty people, there’s always something for everyone. Anyone is welcome to come sit at our aesthetically pleasing coffee shop. Might be in a hurry? Get a takeaway! Planning to make a party? We offer sharing boxes of mojito’s, iced tea, sandwiches, family boxes, and many more!
